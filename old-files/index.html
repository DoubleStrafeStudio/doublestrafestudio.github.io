TinyText Converter

Sometimes normal text doesn't convey the message of what you want to say properly and you just need that diminutive font size to help characterize your thoughts. Unfortunately, that's not possible in most instant messaging environments.


With the help of unicode symbols that are smaller versions of latin alphabet letters, this converter can help you with that! It will take in your typed message and convert it to a smaller version to make your message that much cuter, convey that meek tone of voice, or whatever else an adorably smaller font might help you say.

Keyboard Visualizer

This three.js-based graphical application is here to help keyboard enthusiasts vizualise their dream keyboards. Custom mechanical keyboards are too expensive to be left wondering what it will look like 2 years and $500+ later. This visualizer will let help you see it before you commit to it buying that case or those keycaps
